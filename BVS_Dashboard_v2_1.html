🔐 Admin sign-in

Portfolio editing (Apps, revenue goal, expenses, marketing, versions, exports) is locked until you sign in here. Without signing in, the dashboard is view-only. Session lasts until you close the browser tab (about 12 hours). While signed in, changes are auto-saved to this browser (localStorage). If BVS_CLOUD_API_BASE points at your Vercel deployment (same repo), edits also sync to the cloud after login.

Tip: Change the password by editing ADMIN_PASS_HASH_HEX in js/bvs-dashboard.js (SHA-256 hex). See README.
